What makes us Different

Local and Independent

As a local and independent business, we take great pride in serving our community. We are not part of a large chain or corporation, which allows us to provide a more personalised and tailored approach to hearing care. We are deeply rooted in the local community, and our focus is solely on meeting the needs of our clients. Being local and independent means that we have the flexibility to make decisions based on what is best for our clients, ensuring a high level of customer satisfaction.

Years of Experience

With 13 years of experience working in the hearing care industry, our team has acquired extensive knowledge and expertise in helping individuals with hearing loss. We have witnessed the advancements and innovations in hearing aid technology and tinnitus management over the years. This experience enables us to offer the latest and most effective solutions to our clients. Our longevity in the industry is a testament to our commitment to providing exceptional care and continuously staying up to date with advancements in the field.

Personalised Approach

At our clinic, we believe in a personalised approach to hearing care. We understand that each individual has unique hearing needs and preferences. Our team takes the time to get to know our clients, their hearing concerns, and their lifestyle. We then tailor our services and recommendations accordingly. Whether it’s finding the right hearing aid, fitting ear plugs, conducting a hearing assessment, or managing tinnitus, we prioritise individualised care to ensure the best possible outcomes for our clients.

These three factors set us apart and make our business special
