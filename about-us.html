About_us

Local and Independent Audiologist!

Thank you for considering Hunter Valley Audiology for your hearing healthcare needs.

Hunter Valley Audiology was established by experienced Clinical Audiologist Jaivikas Gowda (Jai). For Over 13 years, Jai has worked across Australia, majorly in the Nation’s Capital (Canberra). Before starting this venture, Jai went on a spiritual journey that included community volunteering, yoga, and meditation with the Isha Foundation in southern India. This experience has helped him become a fantastic audiologist.

I am passionate about giving back to the community and find great joy in helping people improve their hearing health. I aim to raise awareness and provide access to high-quality, family-focused hearing care in our local communities. We want to make it available to everyone who needs it. We hope to positively impact our clients’ and communities’ hearing health by delivering exceptional services and personalised care.

I have called Australia home since 2011. After completing my University training in 2010 with a Master’s degree in Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology, my passion for Audiology inspired me to focus solely on it and start my practice. After working for over a decade for corporate companies, I understand the importance of personalised care, and my goal is to provide customised services that meet the needs and circumstances of each client.

WHAT MAKES THE BUSINESS SPECIAL

Local and Independent

As a local and independent business, we take great pride in serving our community. We are not part of a large chain or corporation, which allows us to provide a more personalized and tailored approach to hearing care. We are deeply rooted in the local community, and our focus is solely on meeting the needs of our clients. Being local and independent means that we have the flexibility to make decisions based on what is best for our clients, ensuring a high level of customer satisfaction.

Years of Experience

With 13 years of experience working in the hearing care industry, our team has acquired extensive knowledge and expertise in helping individuals with hearing loss. We have witnessed the advancements and innovations in hearing aid technology and tinnitus management over the years. This experience enables us to offer the latest and most effective solutions to our clients. Our longevity in the industry is a testament to our commitment to providing exceptional care and continuously staying up to date with advancements in the field.

Personalised Approach

At our clinic, we believe in a personalised approach to hearing care. We understand that each individual has unique hearing needs and preferences. Our team takes the time to get to know our clients, their hearing concerns, and their lifestyle. We then tailor our services and recommendations accordingly. Whether it’s finding the right hearing aid, fitting ear plugs, conducting a hearing assessment, or managing tinnitus, we prioritise individualised care to ensure the best possible outcomes for our clients. These three factors – being local and independent, having years of experience, and offering a personalised approach – set us apart and make our services special. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and improving the quality of life for those who put their trust in us.
